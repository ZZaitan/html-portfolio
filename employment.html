Employment

Thorntons Leamington Spa

November 2016 - April 2019

I worked for nearly 2 years at Thorntons as a sales assistant before training up to the position of senior sales in August 2018. My role as a sales assistant required me to interact with customers in a variety of ways, from greeting them upon entry to the shop, to answering questions about our products and services. As part of my senior sales position my responsibilities included both opening and closing the shop alone, counting money, banking and various stock management jobs. Most of my shifts as senior sales assistant required me to be the supervisor for that day, being responsible for the staff and the shop. As part of my training, I have also received a level 2 certificate in food safety, issued internally by the company.

For 3 months there was a transition period between managers, and I was asked to be acting manager during this time. My time as acting manager allowed me to learn various managerial skills such as creating a weekly rota for an entire team, processing the wages of each team member, and amending the weekly stock order. It also gave me a chance to work on my team-building and motivational skills, as well as effective time management of both myself and the rest of the team. The shop also experiences monthly mystery shopper reports, one of which I was solely responsible for achieving 100% in.
